The Pod Library is where you discover what others have built. Instead of starting from scratch, you can browse, add, and customize existing pods.

## Browsing the Library

Open the Pod Library from your desktop to see available pods organized by category:

- **Productivity** - Task managers, note-taking, calendars
- **Entertainment** - Games, media, fun stuff
- **Travel** - Trip planning, itineraries, booking helpers
- **Sports** - Scores, trackers, fitness tools
- **Business** - CRM, invoicing, analytics
- **Lifestyle** - Habits, recipes, personal tools

Each pod shows a preview, description, and what integrations it uses.

## Editing Pods

Want to customize a pod? Edit it to create your own version.

When you edit a pod:
- You get a complete copy you can modify
- The original stays unchanged
- Your changes are yours to keep or share

This is how software compounds in DeepSpace. Someone builds a basic task tracker. You edit it and add priority levels. Someone else edits yours and adds team assignments. Each version builds on the last.

## Finding the Right Pod

- **Search** by keyword or functionality
- **Filter** by category or integration
- **Sort** by popularity, recency, or relevance

Can't find what you need? That's where [building your own](/getting-started/building-pods) comes in.
