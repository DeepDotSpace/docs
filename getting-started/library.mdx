title: "The Library"
description: "Browse and add widgets, themes, and spaces from the community"
---

The Library is where you discover what the DeepSpace community has built. It has three tabs: **Widgets**, **Themes**, and **Spaces**.

Open the Library from your space to browse, search, and add things to your workspace.

## Widgets

The Widgets tab is where you find ready-made mini-apps. Browse by category — Productivity, Entertainment, Education, Business, and more — or search for something specific.

Each widget shows a preview and description, so you know what it does before adding it.

**Adding a widget:** Click to add it to your space. It appears right away — no installation, no setup.

**Customizing a widget:** Every widget can be edited. When you do:
- You get your own copy to modify
- The original stays untouched for others
- Your version is yours to keep, tweak further, or share back

This is how ideas build on each other. Someone creates a basic expense tracker. You add categories and charts. Someone else takes your version and adds receipt scanning. Each version evolves from the last.

You can also sort by **Trending**, **Top Rated**, **Newest**, or **Name**, and filter to show only your favorites or widgets you've created.

## Themes

Themes control the visual style of your space — colors, backgrounds, and overall atmosphere.

**Browsing themes:** Each theme card shows a color preview and background image so you can see what it looks like before applying it.

**Applying a theme:** Click **Apply** on any theme. Your space updates instantly.

**Creating a theme:** Click **Create Theme** and upload an image or video. The AI generates a matching color palette automatically. You can adjust the colors, give it a name, and publish it for others to use.

## Spaces

Spaces are reusable workspace templates. Instead of setting up widgets and layouts from scratch, you can import a pre-built space.

**Browsing spaces:** See what others have shared — complete workspace setups with widgets already arranged and configured.

**Importing a space:** Click to add a space to your canvas. It brings in the layout, widgets, and configuration.

**Publishing your space:** If you've built a workspace you're proud of, you can publish it to the library for others to discover and use.

**Adding a blank space:** You can also create a fresh, empty space from the Spaces tab.

## Finding What You Need

All three tabs support:
- **Search** by keyword
- **Sort** by trending, rating, newest, or name
- **Favorites** to bookmark things you like
- **Voting** to surface the best content
