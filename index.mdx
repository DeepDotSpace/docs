title: "Welcome to DeepSpace"
---

DeepSpace is a platform where you can build, discover, and share interactive apps — all without writing code. Describe what you want in plain language, and the AI builds it for you.

Whether you need a habit tracker, a sales dashboard, a trip planner, or a game, you can have it running in minutes.

## Key Concepts

### Spaces

A space is your workspace — it's where everything lives. When you open DeepSpace, you're in a space.

There are two types:

- **Desktop** — A fixed-viewport layout where widgets are arranged in a dock. Great for dashboards, daily tools, and structured setups.
- **Canvas** — A free-form infinite canvas you can pan and zoom. Great for brainstorming, visual layouts, and larger projects.

You can create multiple spaces and switch between them. The Library also has a **Spaces** tab where you can browse and import spaces others have shared.

### Widgets

Widgets are the core of DeepSpace. Each widget is a self-contained mini-app — a task tracker, a calculator, a dashboard, a game, anything you need.

You can:
- **Browse** the library and add widgets others have built
- **Build** your own by describing what you want to the AI
- **Customize** existing widgets to fit your needs
- **Share** what you create with the community
- **Deploy** a widget as a standalone website

Widgets store data automatically — anything you add persists across sessions without you having to think about it.

### Themes

Themes control the look and feel of your space. You can browse community-created themes in the Library, or create your own by uploading an image or video — the AI generates a matching color palette automatically.

### Deepspace Agent

Deepspace Agent is your builder. Open the chat and tell it what you want in everyday language — "make me a workout tracker" or "build a dashboard for my sales data" — and it creates a working widget for you. You can keep refining it through conversation until it's exactly right.

When you first open the chat, you'll see a set of suggestions to help you get started.
